About the Firm

Where structured methodology meets real-world impact

Nine Arch Group is a professional governance, risk, and digital transformation advisory firm serving government institutions, development organisations, and private sector enterprises across Sri Lanka and internationally. Our approach is rooted in internationally recognised frameworks — ISO, IIA, and COSO standards — adapted to each client's operational reality and regulatory context.

Our practice is led by professionals with cross-sector experience spanning military command, corporate compliance, executive operations, and active professional audit practice. That rare combination of disciplines produces advisory that is analytically rigorous and operationally grounded — not generic, and not delegated.

The firm is named for Sri Lanka's iconic Nine Arch Bridge in Ella — a structure celebrated not for its appearance, but for the precision engineering that makes it endure. That principle defines how we build governance frameworks: structures that hold under scrutiny, not just on paper.

We do not deliver generic recommendations. Every engagement is scoped with specific deliverables, defined timelines, and outcomes measured against agreed indicators — so clients know exactly what they are getting, and can hold us to it.

A practice built on
three disciplines, not one

Malith Gunasinghe founded Nine Arch Group to address a gap in the advisory market: organisations needed governance, risk, and digital advisory that combined academic rigour with genuine operational experience — not consultants who had only ever worked in consultancy.

Commissioned as a Captain in the Sri Lanka Army, Malith built a decade of decision-making discipline under real operational pressure — leading teams, managing uncertainty, and delivering structured outcomes in demanding environments. That foundation informs every client engagement: clarity of scope, precision in execution, accountability for results.

In parallel, Malith has built a rigorous professional practice in internal audit and governance consulting at Baker Tilly Sri Lanka, conducting 90+ audits across financial, operational, compliance, and ISO framework environments. His work spans GRC, cybersecurity risk, health & safety management systems, and supply chain security — each domain grounded in verifiable international standards. Earlier career roles at MAS Holdings and Vigilant Security & Investigations (Pvt) Ltd further deepened his cross-sector experience across large-scale manufacturing operations and security risk management.

Academic pursuit of an MBA in E-Governance at Kotelawala Defence University ensures the practice stays current with evolving public sector digital governance thinking — bridging theory and operational reality in a way that most advisory practices do not.

Our Approach

A structured engagement model,
not open-ended advisory

Every Nine Arch Group engagement follows a defined four-phase model. You know what we deliver, when we deliver it, and how success is measured — before work begins.

01

Discovery & Scoping

We assess your current governance posture, regulatory obligations, and priority risk areas through structured interviews and documentation review. Output: a scoped engagement proposal with fixed deliverables.

02

Framework Design

We design the governance, audit, or transformation framework aligned to ISO standards, regulatory requirements, and your operational context. Every recommendation is traceable to evidence and applicable standards.

03

Implementation Support

We remain engaged through implementation — not just report delivery. Progress is measured against agreed indicators, with practical guidance for your team at every stage of the change process.

04

Review & Assurance

We validate that governance improvements are embedded in operations through structured review — producing an assurance report suitable for board, donor, or regulatory audiences.
